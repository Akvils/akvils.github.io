Memento Mori

Remember you must Die.

Watch trailer on youtube

I designed an immersive 3D VR data visualization exploring unnatural deaths in India from 2001 to 2013. Users enter this interactive experience to explore the data by selecting a cause of death, immersing themselves in the corresponding scenario. The journey takes them from the realm of the living to the void, allowing them to navigate data as a spirit. Here, they encounter the god of transformation, offering a new birth to experience death once more. This VR experience bridges data and spirituality, providing users with a profound sense of reincarnation through the lens of data.

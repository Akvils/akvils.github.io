Explorations of Existence:
7 Short Films

These are my seven short films, each delving into different existential principles and storytelling techniques. 'PaperBoat' reflects on rebirths of spirits, 'Dialogue' humorously narrates a life journey, 'Hope' is a prayer of hope, 'Deja vu' explores karmic repetitive rebirths, 'Ugly Show' depicts life as a stage, 'Love' distinguishes love as a way of being versus attachment, and '9 Months' contemplates existence before human birth.

Role
Storytelling, Animation, Video Editing, Management

  • PaperBoat is a short film about meditative acceptance of a young boy who is having dialogue with his dead sister after knowing that his father has been hanged. It is a exploration on the principle of rebirths of spirits.

  • Dialogue, is a monologue of a mans humorous life journey.

  • Hope, is a prayer.

  • Deja vu' is a experimental short film exploring the principle of karmic repetitive rebirths.

  • Ugly show, represents life as a stage and how fundamentally human existence is suffering.

  • Love, explores the distinction of love as way of being versus attachment to a concept of it.

  • 9 months, explores the existence of being before one shows up as a human being.

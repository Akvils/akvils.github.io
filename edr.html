Malwarebytes EDR

malwarebytes.com/edr

In the ever-evolving landscape of cybersecurity, I embarked on a transformative journey to reshape and enhance EDR (Endpoint Detection and Response). My role in this project was to champion user-centric design, simplifying complexity, and making this powerful tool accessible to analysts. As a result, we achieved remarkable growth, with the user base surging from practically zero to 1.2 million users in just seven quarters. This is the story of how we turned a challenging landscape into a user-friendly terrain, empowering analysts to safeguard digital security effectively.

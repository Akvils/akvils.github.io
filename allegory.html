The Allegory

Complete Experience

Through virtual reality (VR), I crafted 'The Allegory,' an immersive experience inspired by Plato's 'Allegory of the Cave.' In this VR journey, users step into the shoes of the cave prisoner, venturing into a world of shifting realities. 'The Allegory' unfolds as a series of captivating events that mirror the evolution of human perception, offering an experiential exploration of the journey towards enlightenment.
